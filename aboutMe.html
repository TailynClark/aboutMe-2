Tailyn Clark

Tailyn Clark is a Photographer and Designer based in the Southeastern area of Massachusetts. Her focus lies in branding and editorial design. She has experienced in working with local businesses of all sizes to create a culturally relevant atmosphere. Tailyn is currently working as Chief Photographer of PhotoGraphics and Marketing Department at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. Tailyn also freelances, working with models and musicians, wedding and event photography.
